Rules and Play

Select 2 cards
Match: remove them and go again
No match: next players turn

Regular Scoring

Card first show: 1pt
First match: 5pts
Streak: Matches this turn
Matches: 2pts + 2pts * Streak

Stable Scoring

Card first show: 1pt
Matches: 2pts

Simple Scoring

Matches: 1pt

Winner

Most points, in case of a tie - player last in the turn order wins.
